About Me

Hello my name is Terry Swanson and I currently live in Martinborough. Throughout my years of being a chef I have about 25 years experience of being an executive chef. I have previously operated in my own resturant for ten years called Cobar in Days Bay, Eastbourne, New Zealand. Being part of the Cobar has being a great pleasure of mine.

The popular services that I provide include me coming to your home to make home cooked meals. I can cater for up to twenty people at once.

For a list of what I can cook please refer to my Menu's Page where it will display all my dishes and everything else that I can provide for you. If you still need further details then please refer to my Contacts Page where you can get in contact with me.

~Terry Swanson, Executive Chef.
